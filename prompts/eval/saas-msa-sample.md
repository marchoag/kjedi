# MASTER SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT

This Master Subscription Agreement (this "**Agreement**") is entered into as of April 1, 2026 (the "**Effective Date**") between **Helios Analytics, Inc.**, a Delaware corporation ("**Vendor**"), and **Acme Robotics, Inc.**, a Delaware corporation ("**Customer**").

## 1. Services

1.1 **Subscription Services.** Subject to the terms of this Agreement, Vendor will make available to Customer the cloud-based analytics platform identified in the applicable Order Form (the "**Services**").

1.2 **Order Forms.** Customer may purchase Services pursuant to one or more order forms executed by both Parties (each, an "**Order Form**"). Each Order Form is incorporated by reference into this Agreement.

## 2. Use of the Services

2.1 **License.** Subject to Customer's compliance with this Agreement and payment of all applicable Fees, Vendor grants Customer a non-exclusive, non-transferable right to access and use the Services during the Term solely for Customer's internal business purposes.

2.2 **Restrictions.** Customer shall not, and shall not permit any third party to: (a) reverse engineer, decompile, or disassemble the Services; (b) modify, translate, or create derivative works of the Services; (c) use the Services to develop a competing product; (d) circumvent any security measure of the Services; or (e) use the Services in violation of applicable law.

## 3. Fees and Payment

3.1 **Fees.** Customer shall pay the fees set forth in each Order Form (the "**Fees**"). All Fees are non-cancelable and non-refundable except as expressly set forth herein.

3.2 **Invoicing and Payment.** Vendor will invoice Customer annually in advance. Customer shall pay all undisputed invoiced amounts within thirty (30) days of receipt of invoice. Late payments shall accrue interest at the rate of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is lower.

## 4. Term and Termination

4.1 **Term.** This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue until all Order Forms have expired or been terminated. Each Order Form shall have an initial term of twelve (12) months from the date of execution (the "**Initial Term**").

4.2 **Auto-Renewal.** Each Order Form will automatically renew for successive twelve (12)-month renewal terms (each, a "**Renewal Term**" and together with the Initial Term, the "**Term**") unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal **at least one hundred twenty (120) days prior to the end of the then-current Term**. Upon renewal, Vendor may increase the Fees by up to **fifteen percent (15%)** without further notice or consent.

4.3 **Termination for Cause.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement upon thirty (30) days' written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within such notice period.

## 5. Service Level Agreement

5.1 **Uptime Commitment.** Vendor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to make the Services available 99.0% of the time during each calendar month, excluding scheduled maintenance and force majeure events.

5.2 **Remedies.** **Customer's sole and exclusive remedy for any failure to meet the uptime commitment is the right to submit a written request for service credits, which Vendor shall consider in good faith. Vendor's decision regarding service credits shall be final and binding.**

## 6. Data

6.1 **Customer Data.** As between the Parties, Customer retains all right, title, and interest in and to Customer's data submitted to or processed by the Services ("**Customer Data**"). Customer grants Vendor a limited license to use Customer Data solely as necessary to provide the Services.

6.2 **Data Processing.** The Parties' processing of personal data shall be governed by the Data Processing Addendum (the "**DPA**") **to be executed as a separate document**.

6.3 **Aggregated Data.** Vendor may use anonymized and aggregated data derived from Customer Data for any purpose, including improving the Services and benchmarking.

## 7. Intellectual Property

7.1 **Vendor IP.** Vendor retains all right, title, and interest in and to the Services and all related intellectual property rights.

7.2 **Feedback.** Customer hereby assigns to Vendor any feedback, suggestions, or improvements provided by Customer regarding the Services.

## 8. Indemnification

8.1 **By Vendor.** Vendor shall defend Customer against any third-party claim alleging that Customer's use of the Services in accordance with this Agreement infringes such third party's intellectual property rights, and shall indemnify Customer against any damages finally awarded by a court of competent jurisdiction or paid in settlement of such claim. **Vendor's total liability under this Section 8.1 shall be unlimited.**

8.2 **By Customer.** Customer shall defend Vendor against any third-party claim arising out of Customer's breach of Section 2.2 or Customer's misuse of the Services, and shall indemnify Vendor against any damages finally awarded by a court of competent jurisdiction or paid in settlement of such claim.

## 9. Limitation of Liability

EXCEPT FOR EACH PARTY'S INDEMNIFICATION OBLIGATIONS UNDER SECTION 8 AND BREACHES OF CONFIDENTIALITY OBLIGATIONS, IN NO EVENT SHALL EITHER PARTY'S AGGREGATE LIABILITY UNDER THIS AGREEMENT EXCEED THE FEES PAID OR PAYABLE BY CUSTOMER TO VENDOR IN THE TWELVE (12) MONTHS PRECEDING THE EVENT GIVING RISE TO LIABILITY.

## 10. Confidentiality

Each Party shall hold the other Party's Confidential Information in strict confidence and shall not disclose it to any third party without the prior written consent of the other Party. The receiving Party shall use the same degree of care to protect such Confidential Information as it uses to protect its own, but in no event less than reasonable care.

## 11. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California, without regard to its conflict of laws principles. The Parties consent to the exclusive jurisdiction of the state and federal courts located in San Francisco County, California.

## 12. General

12.1 **Notices.** All notices shall be in writing and delivered to the addresses set forth in the applicable Order Form.

12.2 **Force Majeure.** Neither Party shall be liable for any failure or delay in performance due to causes beyond its reasonable control.

12.3 **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement, together with all Order Forms, constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties.

12.4 **Counterparts.** This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original.
