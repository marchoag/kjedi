Dear Jamie,

We are pleased to offer you the position of **Senior Software Engineer** at Acme Robotics, Inc. ("**Acme**" or the "**Company**"), reporting to the VP of Engineering. Your start date will be **June 1, 2026**.

## 1. Compensation

1.1 **Base Salary.** Your initial annual base salary will be **$220,000**, paid in accordance with the Company's standard payroll practices.

1.2 **Equity.** Subject to approval by the Board of Directors, you will be granted an option to purchase **40,000 shares** of the Company's common stock (the "**Option**"). The Option will vest over **four (4) years**, with **25% vesting on the one-year anniversary** of your start date and the remainder vesting in equal monthly installments thereafter, subject to your continued service.

1.3 **Bonus.** You will be eligible for an annual discretionary bonus of up to **15%** of your base salary, payable based on individual and Company performance.

## 2. Benefits

You will be eligible to participate in Acme's standard employee benefits programs, including health, dental, and vision insurance, 401(k) plan, and unlimited paid time off, subject to the terms of each plan.

## 3. At-Will Employment

Your employment with Acme is **at-will**, meaning that either you or the Company may terminate the employment relationship at any time, with or without cause and with or without notice. This at-will employment relationship may not be modified except in a writing signed by an authorized officer of the Company.

## 4. Severance

If your employment is terminated by the Company without Cause (as defined below) or by you for Good Reason (as defined below), and subject to your execution of a release of claims, you will be entitled to: (a) **three (3) months** of your then-current base salary, paid in a lump sum; and (b) **three (3) months** of accelerated vesting of the Option.

"**Cause**" means (i) your conviction of a felony, (ii) your gross misconduct, or (iii) your material breach of any agreement with the Company.

"**Good Reason**" means a material reduction in your base salary or a material adverse change in your duties, in each case without your consent.

## 5. Confidentiality and Inventions Assignment

As a condition of your employment, you must sign Acme's standard Confidentiality and Inventions Assignment Agreement (the "**CIAA**"), a copy of which is attached as **Exhibit A**.

Pursuant to the CIAA, you agree to assign to Acme all right, title, and interest in any **inventions, discoveries, or works of authorship** that you conceive, develop, or reduce to practice during the term of your employment, **whether or not** such inventions are made (a) using Company resources, (b) during working hours, or (c) within the scope of your employment.

## 6. Non-Competition

For a period of **twelve (12) months** following the termination of your employment with Acme for any reason, you agree that you will not, directly or indirectly, engage in, own, manage, operate, control, be employed by, or participate in the ownership, management, operation, or control of any business that competes with Acme anywhere in the United States.

## 7. Non-Solicitation

For a period of **eighteen (18) months** following the termination of your employment with Acme, you agree that you will not, directly or indirectly:

(a) solicit, recruit, or hire any employee, consultant, or contractor of Acme; or

(b) solicit any customer or prospective customer of Acme with whom you had contact during your employment.

## 8. Background Check

This offer is contingent upon (a) the successful completion of a background check; (b) your verification of legal authorization to work in the United States; and (c) your execution of the CIAA.

## 9. Governing Law

This offer letter and your employment shall be governed by the laws of the **State of California**.

## 10. Entire Agreement

This offer letter, together with the CIAA, constitutes the entire agreement between you and Acme regarding the terms of your employment and supersedes all prior agreements or understandings.

To accept this offer, please sign and return this letter by **May 15, 2026**.
